Goal and Scope

The goal for the future site is to be my portfolio website, that will showcase the projects that I have worked on. My audience will be people I give my resume to (recruiters, companies, etc.). Due to this, I intend to make the main page much like a cover letter, outlining strengths and projects. Then, or throughout, I will have links to pages about specific topics, if they are large enough to warrant it. (Honestly maybe one at the moment.)

Additional Notes

Some challenging parts for phase 1 were dealing with the shifting aspect ratio and spacing. There were several states where the majority of the text filled too much space or not enough depending on the width of the screen. I was working on making the wide version look good enough at a couple different screens and it involved shifting the sticky note and even changing the font size on really big screens. Also, centering the text on the sticky note at multiple sizes was a big hassle, and I still do not think it looks right sometimes.
