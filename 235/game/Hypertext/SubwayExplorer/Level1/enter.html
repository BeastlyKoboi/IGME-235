Enter The Subway

You enter the subway splattered in rain. You quickly adjust to the light levels and make your way further towards where the mural was. You begin to hear slight squishing, light pitter patter, and a strange SCREECH, from a different direction from where you entered. This seemed strange to you, as you remember that the entrance that way was covered along time ago. At the same time you can finally make out a feeble ray of light near where you were headed. Curiousity naws at the back of your mind.
