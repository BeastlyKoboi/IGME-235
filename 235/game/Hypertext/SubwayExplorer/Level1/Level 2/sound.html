Follow The Sound

Curiousity getting the best of you, you divert your course towards the noise you hear. It steadily gets louder and louder as you make your way forward. The light of your lantern finally reveals a covered section of the tunnel, inside of which lies a staircase. A staircase which supposedly led nowhere, yet had the unmistakeable sounds of footsteps and the screeching of a train. You could sense the air was less stale here than before and that both chilled and excited you.
