Go To The Light

Determined to find that mural and view it in all its glory you ignore the sound and head forward. As you head towards the light, you find the blurry outline of people. Two people crowding around a wall with buckets. You get within speaking distance and see they also have large painter's brushes. You also see that they are young, practically teenagers. You see around them, drawn haphazardly are phalluses, still wet from creation. A quick "Hey" from you was all it took to send them running, though you don't know why. Proof of their youth, that they believed this was the height of criminality. Though certainly a crime in your eyes. Fortunately the mural you came to photograph was spared. You stop to admire it, before finally taking a photo. You head back, no noise to divert you this time and go back to your car, sun now shining and turn the key which started the car with no objections.
