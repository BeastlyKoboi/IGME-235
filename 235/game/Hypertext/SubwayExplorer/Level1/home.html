Home Bound

You heave a big sigh while looking at the entrance, and turn away. You decide to wait for a day where you won't be likely to trip once inside. Quickly, you make a dash back to your car. Once there you dry off inside and try to start the car. A car which refuses to start. A problem you have been having recently, which slips your mind every time it works as intended. You decide to call your friend to pick you up, but it looks like it'll be a while until they arrive. After the call you consider entering the subway as you originally intended or just waiting in the car, so you don't get soaked even more.
