Subway Explorer

The sky is a howling torrent and the sun is nowhere to be seen. You walk along the gravel with a oversized poncho covering you and a bright lantern guiding you, towards the opening of the subway ahead. Despite the poor conditions your steps are resolute and eyes determined, because you had something to see. A lovely mural that you had the pleasure of viewing only once before was stuck in your head, and nowhere else since your photo got corrupted somehow. Today was the only free day you have for a while and you decided it was time to revisit the mural for another photo. You pause and glance at the sky. A moment of indecision strikes and you contemplate just going home now.
